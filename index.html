Features

  • Instant Emergency Reporting: Users can quickly send SOS alerts with essential details like type, location, and time.
  • Live Incident Dashboard: Staff can monitor all incoming emergencies in real time from a centralized dashboard.
  • Smart Dispatch System: Emergencies are assigned to the most suitable and available response units for faster action.
  • Location-Based Coordination: Ensures accurate tracking and efficient allocation of resources based on proximity.
  • Instant Notifications: Alerts staff immediately when a new emergency is reported.
  • Emergency History & Records: Maintains a record of past incidents for analysis and future improvements.
